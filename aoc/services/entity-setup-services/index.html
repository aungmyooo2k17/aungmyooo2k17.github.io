Setup of Various Entities

We provide comprehensive services for establishing various business structures tailored to your specific needs, ensuring compliance with all regulatory requirements while optimizing your tax position.

1. Company Setup:

  • ✅
    Incorporation & Registration – We handle all aspects of company formation, including registering with the Australian Securities & Investments Commission (ASIC) and obtaining an Australian Business Number (ABN) and Tax File Number (TFN).
  • ✅
    Corporate Structure Advice – We provide advice on the shareholder and director structure, ensuring your company setup aligns with your business goals.
  • ✅
    Company Constitution & Shareholder Agreements – Drafting a company constitution and assisting with shareholder agreements to ensure clear terms for decision-making and profit distribution.
  • ✅
    Compliance Setup – We ensure that your company is fully compliant with corporate laws, tax regulations, and reporting obligations.

2. Trust Setup (Family, Unit, Discretionary):

  • ✅
    Family Trust Setup – Setting up a discretionary family trust allows you to protect your wealth and distribute income in a tax-effective manner. We ensure that your trust deed is properly tailored to meet your family's needs.
  • ✅
    Unit Trust Setup – Ideal for joint ventures or property investments, a unit trust provides clear ownership of assets based on units held. We assist in the formation and structuring of unit trusts for investment or business purposes.
  • ✅
    Trust Deed & Trustee Advice – We prepare a trust deed that defines the terms of your trust, ensuring it's structured to maximize flexibility and minimize tax obligations.
  • ✅
    Compliance & Reporting – Ongoing advice on trust distributions, tax implications, and reporting requirements for your trust.

3. Partnership Setup:

  • ✅
    Partnership Agreement & Structuring – We help you create a partnership agreement that outlines the roles, responsibilities, and profit-sharing arrangements between partners, providing clarity and minimizing disputes.
  • ✅
    Tax Considerations & Liability Protection – Advice on tax-effective partnership structures and strategies to protect your personal assets.
  • ✅
    Registration & Compliance – Assistance with registering your partnership with the appropriate authorities and ensuring compliance with partnership tax obligations.

4. Self-Managed Superannuation Fund (SMSF) Setup:

  • ✅
    Establishment of SMSF – We assist in establishing your own SMSF, including preparing the trust deed, registering with the ATO, and ensuring that the structure is compliant with superannuation laws.
  • ✅
    Trustee Structure Advice – Guidance on selecting the right trustee structure (individual trustees vs. corporate trustee) based on your financial goals.
  • ✅
    Investment Strategy & Compliance – We work with you to create an SMSF investment strategy that aligns with your retirement goals and complies with superannuation regulations.
