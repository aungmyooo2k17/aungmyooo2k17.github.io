About AOC Accounting & Tax

Our Expertise

At AOC Accounting & Tax, we are dedicated to providing exceptional accounting services to individuals and businesses. The director Andrea O'Connor, brings over 25 years of experience in both Australian and international accounting.

With Masters in Taxation and Business, a Masters in Economics, and qualifications as a CPA, Tax Agent, and ASIC Agent, I am passionate about delivering personalized, high-quality financial solutions tailored to your unique needs.

Every client is different, and we pride ourselves on offering customized services to help you meet your specific financial goals. Whether you're seeking tax planning, business structuring, or expert financial reporting, we ensure your finances are optimized for both growth and tax efficiency.
